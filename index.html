Questions

Everything you want to know.

Yes, completely. Colors costs nothing. You need an AI API key to power conversations — Anthropic gives $5 free credit on signup, OpenAI has a free tier too. Or use a local model with LM Studio at zero ongoing cost.
ChatGPT stores your conversations on OpenAI's servers. Colors runs on your machine. Nothing stored remotely. The AI still comes from Anthropic or OpenAI — but your history, memory, and files stay on your computer, encrypted, readable only by you.
Colors remembers things about you — your name, preferences, projects — so you don't repeat yourself every session. That memory is saved in a locked file on your computer. Only your passphrase unlocks it. Even if someone stole your laptop, they couldn't read it.
On Windows you download a file and double-click it. On Mac and Linux you paste one command into a terminal. If you've installed software before, you can install Colors. We're actively making this even simpler.
Hackers hide instructions inside webpages or documents. When a normal AI reads that page, it follows those hidden instructions — sending your files, leaking data, doing things you never asked. This is called prompt injection. Colors tracks where every instruction came from. External content can't give Colors orders. Only you can.
Anthropic (Claude), OpenAI (GPT-4), Groq, Ollama, and LM Studio for fully local free models. You choose the AI. Colors handles the privacy, memory, and security layer on top.
